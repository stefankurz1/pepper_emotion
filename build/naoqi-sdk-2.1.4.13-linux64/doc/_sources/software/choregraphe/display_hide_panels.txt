.. _customizing_layout:

Choregraphe layout
======================

.. _choregraphe-howto-display-hide-panels:

Displaying / hiding panels
--------------------------

From the **View** menu, click the panel to display or hide.

.. image:: /medias/desktop/choregraphe/view_menu.png
   :width: 107 px
   :height: 285 px

Each panel that is already displayed is marked.


.. _choregraphe-howto-move-panels:

Moving panels
---------------

To move a panel, drag it by its title bar.

**Tip**: to prevent a panel to dock automatically,
**press Control** before dragging it.

According to the place you dragged it, the panel can:

* Take another corner of the **Choregraphe** window.

  .. image:: /medias/desktop/choregraphe/panel_corner.png
     :width: 404 px
     :height: 194 px

* Overlap another panel.
  Tabs appear in order to let you switch between the 2 panels.

  .. image:: /medias/desktop/choregraphe/panel_overlap.png
     :width: 404 px
     :height: 176 px

Click the **resize** button to obtain a floating window.

.. image:: /medias/desktop/choregraphe/panel_floating.png
   :width: 276 px
   :height: 181 px

.. _save_layouts:

Saving and loading Layouts
----------------------------

To save the current position of all panels:

* Choose **View** > **Save Layout...**

To display the panels as you previously saved them:

* Choose **View** > **Load Layout...**

To come back to the default **Layout**:

* Choose **View** > **Reset Views**.
