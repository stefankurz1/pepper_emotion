Retrieving software
====================

.. Warning::
   You do not have an |robot| yet?

   You will not be able to download any software except **Choregraphe**
   trial version.

To download software from |community|:

=====  =========================================================================
Step       Action
=====  =========================================================================
1.      Make sure your |roboN| is registered on your |community_account|.

        For further details, see: :ref:`registering_robot_nao`.

2.      Access the software resources of |community|:

        |community_software_link|

3.      Sign in, using your |community_account| credentials.

        All software available are displayed.

        .. image:: /medias/dev/community_sofware.png
            :width: 489 px
            :height: 344 px
=====  =========================================================================
