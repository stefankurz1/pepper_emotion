Progress
=========

For most of the educational **Activities**, performance is recorded.

The **Progress** page allows **Teachers** and **Parents** to display these
performance data on charts.

.. image:: /medias/asknao/progress_page.png
   :width: 487 px
   :height: 384 px

The progress will get updated after each session, each time an **Activity**
provides data.

Selecting an Activity
---------------------

To display the progress made on a specific **Activity**:

* Click the **Activity** icon.

.. image:: /medias/asknao/progress_select_act.png
   :width: 366 px
