Teacher home
=============

The **Teacher** home shares the :ref:`child_tab` with **Parents**.

Additionally, the **Teacher** home displays the:
:ref:`playlist_tab`, :ref:`play_tab`, and :ref:`create_tab`.

Customizing Child Tab
----------------------

The **Child Tab** displays condensed profiles of the children who already have
accounts created.
Children have to be registered through the administration account.

Please provide a list of children names and birthdays to your **ASK NAO Administrator**
and request accounts created under your profile.

To find out who is the **ASK NAO Administrator**:

* Click the **Add a new child** button:

  The error message below displays the names and emails of all the
  **ASK NAO Administrator**.
