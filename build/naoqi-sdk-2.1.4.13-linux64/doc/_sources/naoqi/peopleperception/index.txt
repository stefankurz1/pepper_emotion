--------------------------

This section describes the modules that can be used to analyse the people around the robot.

All robots
++++++++++

+---------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Use ...                         | To ...                                                                        |
+=================================+===============================================================================+
| :ref:`albasicawareness`         | Make the robot react to its environment's stimuli.                            |
+---------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`alengagementzones`        | Analyze the position of a person with respect to the robot.                   |
+---------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`alfacecharacteristics`    | Provide estimated human characteristics based on facial analysis.             |
+---------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`alfacedetection`          | Make the robot detect and recognize human faces.                              |
+---------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`algazeanalysis`           | Analyze the direction of the gaze of a person.                                |
+---------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`alpeopleperception`       | Make the robot keep tracks about the people around it.                        |
+---------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`alsittingpeopledetection` | Make the robot detect whether a person is sitting or not.                     |
+---------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

Requiring a 3D sensor
+++++++++++++++++++++

+---------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Use ...                         | To ...                                                                        |
+=================================+===============================================================================+
| :ref:`alwavingdetection`        | Make the robot detect if a detected person is waving.                         |
