WiFi
------

Main usage
+++++++++++

Communication between **Choregraphe** and the robot.

Specification
++++++++++++++

==========================  ====================================================
 NAO **V5**                  IEEE 802.11 a/b/g/n

 NAO **V4** and **V3.x**     IEEE 802.11 b/g/n
==========================  ====================================================

Security: 64/128 bit: WEP, WPA/WPA2.


USB
-----

Main usage
+++++++++++

Usually used to update the system of the robot, you can also use the **USB** port
to connect external devices such as:

* **Kinect** or **Asus** 3D sensor, or
* **Arduino** device. See an example of implementation provided by our partner:
  http://www.humarobotics.com/en/robotics-lab/nao-and-arduino.html

Location
+++++++++

.. glossary::

   USB port

      To access to the **USB** port, remove the
      :term:`hatch behind the head of the robot`.
