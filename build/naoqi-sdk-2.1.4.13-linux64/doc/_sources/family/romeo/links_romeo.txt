Link definition
----------------

Links are defined using:

* Joint names and,
* a point called **Torso**, located at the trunk of the robot.

Check :ref:`romeo_masses` to visualize them.

**Axis definition**

The **X** axis is positive toward robot's front, the **Y** from right to left
and the **Z** is vertical.

.. image:: /medias/romeo/romeo_xyz.png
   :width: 220 px
   :height: 384 px
